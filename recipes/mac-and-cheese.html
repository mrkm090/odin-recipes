Mac and Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 can condensed Cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 pinch paprika, or as desired

Steps

  1. Fall large pot with lightly salted water and birng to a rolling boil. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and transfer pasta ot a slow cooker.
  2. Add butter to past and stir until melted; season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle about 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.
  3. Whisk evaprated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  4. Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  5. Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook on Low for 2 hours.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
